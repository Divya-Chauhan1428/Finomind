01 — Brand Foundation

Who We Are

FinoMind is a Germany-based financial consulting firm serving the Indian and South Asian diaspora in Europe. We bridge the gap between complex German financial systems and the people who need clear, trustworthy guidance to build their wealth in a new country.

🌍

Market

Germany and wider Europe — home to the largest concentration of Indian professionals abroad, many navigating pensions, tax, and investment for the first time.

👤

Primary Audience

Indian nationals and South Asian diaspora aged 28–50 living in Germany — IT professionals, engineers, doctors, students transitioning to employment.

🎯

Brand Promise

We make German financial life understandable, approachable, and actionable — so every client feels confident, not confused, about their money.

06 — Voice & Tone

How FinoMind Speaks

Our audience has navigated immigration, language barriers, and complex bureaucracy. They deserve clear, respectful communication that treats them as the intelligent adults they are — never condescending, never corporate-cold.

🔍

Clear, not simplistic

Break down financial complexity without dumbing it down. Use plain language and explain terms when they first appear.

"Your Rentenversicherung contributions build your state pension entitlement — here's exactly how much you're accruing."
🤲

Warm, not casual

We are a trusted friend who also happens to be a licensed expert. Professional but never stiff or distant.

"Navigating taxes in a new country isn't easy — we've helped hundreds of Indians in Germany do exactly this."
⚡

Proactive, not pushy

We point out things clients haven't thought of. We never apply pressure or use scarcity tactics.

"Before you file, there's one deduction most new expats miss — let us check your eligibility."
🌏

Culturally aware

Reference shared experiences where relevant: remittances, NRI investments, families back home, long-term Germany residency planning.

"Sending money home? We can help structure this tax-efficiently for both Germany and India."
✓ SAY THIS
  • We'll walk you through each step.
  • Here's what this means for you.
  • You're entitled to claim this.
  • Book a free 30-minute call.
  • Your money, your decision.
✕ AVOID THIS
  • Don't miss this limited offer!
  • As per the applicable legislation…
  • We are pleased to offer synergistic…
  • Act now before it's too late!
  • Our proprietary algorithm guarantees…
Language Guidance
Primary language
English — clear, international English accessible to non-native speakers. Avoid idioms that don't translate.
Secondary language
German — for legal, regulatory, and SEO content. Always pair German terms with English explanations on first use.
Hindi / vernacular
Use sparingly in social content or community campaigns for warmth, never for legal or formal communication.
Numbers & currency
Always use €/EUR for Germany context. Show INR conversions in parentheses where helpful for the audience.
07 — Photography & Imagery

Visual Direction

Photography should feel real and aspirational — not stock-photo generic. Show the actual lives of our clients: South Asian professionals in European cities, families planning their future, confident people at work.

✓ IDEAL IMAGES
  • South Asian professionals in modern European office settings
  • Families in German cities (Frankfurt, Munich, Berlin) environments
  • Candid, natural moments — not posed smiles at cameras
  • Warm, natural light. Colours that complement the teal palette
  • Technology: laptops, phones — modern financial life
  • Architectural details of Germany (buildings, streets)
✕ AVOID
  • Generic "handshake" or "suited businessman" stock images
  • Images with no cultural connection to our audience
  • Oversaturated, heavy filter edits
  • Exclusively white European subjects in all marketing
  • Images that make finance feel intimidating or exclusive
  • Clipart, cartoons, or AI-generated faces
Illustration Style

When illustrations are needed (explainer graphics, icons, infographics), follow these principles:

Colour palette
Strictly from the FinoMind palette. No random accent colours.
Line weight
Consistent 1.5–2px stroke. Clean, geometric forms. Avoid ornate or decorative complexity.
Iconography
Use outline-style icons. Recommended: Phosphor Icons or Heroicons. Never mix styles.
Globe motif
The globe from the logo can be used as a standalone graphic element in illustrations and backgrounds.
08 — Application

Brand in Action

How the brand should appear across key touchpoints — from the website and social media to printed materials and email communications.

Website
Dark authority + cyan accents
  • → Deep teal hero, white body sections
  • → Playfair Display for hero headlines only
  • → Inter 16px / 1.7 line-height for body
  • → Cyan CTAs throughout
  • → Generous whitespace, card-based layout
Social Media
Teal backgrounds, bold data
  • → Square (1:1) or 4:5 portrait format
  • → Large stat or pull-quote as hero element
  • → Always include the logo in bottom-right
  • → LinkedIn: professional, data-driven posts
  • → Instagram: lifestyle, tips, community moments
Email
Clean & scannable
  • → White background, teal header band
  • → Max 600px width
  • → One primary CTA per email
  • → Preheader text always present
  • → Footer in dark teal with unsubscribe link
Print / PDF Reports
Structured & credible
  • → A4 portrait, 20mm margins
  • → Teal header band with logo
  • → Inter for all body copy, 10pt minimum
  • → Tables in Cloud Cyan (#E0F7FC) fills
  • → Page numbers in footer, always
